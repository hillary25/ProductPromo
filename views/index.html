ABOUT US


Founded in October of 2019, Sunnies is a new unique sunglasses brand. Our sunglasses combine aesthetics with function, creating the best pair of shades for all day use. Sunnies is an all inclusive company, designing eyewear for both men and women. Choose from a variety of styles, colours and accessories on our website. We are an online company who ships super quick worldwide. Buy some Sunnies for the sun around you!
